Come to mars, we've got brains

Life on earth is a bust, how about you move to Mars?

Tired of being chased by humans? Tired of being unable to eat brains without being labeled as a monster? Earth is a very judgmental place and not at all suited for the modern zombie. Nowadays you can't even eat brains without an angry horde coming after you with fire torchs.

That's no way to live, don't you think? That's why our leader, Mr. Donovan, has created a colony in Mars where we can run free and eat as many alien brains and we'd like. Sounds too good to be true, doesn't it? Well, it isn't, just come and see for yourself.

I bet you're asking "how can this be possible?" and the answer is quite simple; there are no zombies in Mars, so aliens there have no regulation or forces against us. Also, Mars is a quite peacefull planet who hasn't developed guns of any kind, so you'll be able to eat as many brains as you'd like and no one will be able to anything about it.
